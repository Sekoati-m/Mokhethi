About Us

Ms theatre and fitness club is a gym which is formed and established by me, a Mosotho gentleman from Leribe Maputsoe. My gym is located in Maseru near Limkokwing University of Creative & Technologyand Lerotholi Politechnique. The gym offers nothing but the best services ever with its top-notch Trojan equipment everyone could think a gym should have. I am a young entrepreneur who think mental health and reduced stress are the key components in every student's academic journey, hence my gym is located near the schools so that students can access the gym easily without travelling long distance. I was once a student and i know how painful is like to desire for something like being able to pay for gym but the stipend not being able to cover for that as well as other one's needs, so my gym offers access for students into the gym with the lowest prices ever which matches their stipend.

This gym as already said above, it is willing to accomodate interested students from nearby universities with the lowest prices that match their income bracket, but that does not mean a person who is not a student is not allowed at the gym but the only difference with such people is that they will pay more than the students do for gym because we believe that such people earn more that the students. We are more concerned about the students because we believe that gym could be one of those places whereby one networks with other people, reduces stress, gains stamina and also improves their mental health through the workouts done there. All those things are going to have a positive impact on one's academics and that is going to make Lesotho to have a high literacy rate in which as a result Lesotho will have people who will be able to transform it for the better. For now we are only located near LUCT and FOKOTHI, but in 10 years to come we want to have have braches all across the country where there are institutions of a higher learning such as NUL and Agric College in Leribe. Our aim is to also provide unemployed people with the jobs so that they could be able to put something on the table, save, or pay for their childrens school fees. In some other countries, bodybuilding makes people rich but in Lesotho that is not happening, so we want to also host some competition of bodybuilders quartely in a year so as to boost the morale of bodybuilders in Lesotho and also making them to make living out of their bodies.
